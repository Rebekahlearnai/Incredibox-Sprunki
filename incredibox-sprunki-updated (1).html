The Power of Combinations

In Incredibox Sprunki, the true magic happens when you combine different character types. Start with a rhythm foundation using Drums and Bass, add melodic elements, then layer in Voices and Effects for richness and depth.

Certain combinations will unlock the special Bonus characters which create unique animations and musical flourishes. Experiment with different arrangements to discover all the hidden secrets in the Sprunki universe!

Each character has multiple sound variations to choose from, allowing for countless unique compositions. Mix and match to create your own signature sound and share your masterpiece with friends!
