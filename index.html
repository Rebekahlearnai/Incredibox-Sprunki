How to Play

Incredibox Sprunki is an intuitive music creation game where you can mix different sounds to create your own unique beats.

Simply drag and drop characters onto the stage, each representing different sounds, and watch as they combine to create amazing music.

Experiment with different combinations to unlock special bonuses and discover new musical possibilities!

Features

  • Intuitive drag-and-drop interface
  • Multiple sound characters to mix and match
  • Create, save, and share your compositions
  • Unlock special bonuses and animations
